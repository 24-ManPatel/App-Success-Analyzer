Recommendations

Reviews Matter Most

Apps with 10,000+ reviews are significantly more likely to be rated "Good". Focus on encouraging users to leave reviews early through in-app prompts.

Keep Apps Updated

Apps updated within the last 90 days score higher across all categories. Regular updates signal active maintenance and improve store ranking.

Free Apps Win

Free apps dominate the "Good" tier. If monetizing, use in-app purchases rather than upfront pricing to maximize install volume and ratings.

Install Count is a Strong Signal

Install count is the second most predictive feature. Apps crossing 100K installs have a dramatically higher chance of landing in the "Good" category.
